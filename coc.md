# Code of Conduct (short)

Be excellent to each other. We are an inclusive, professional event.

- Respect everyone; no harassment or discrimination.
- Obtain explicit consent for photos; respect “no photo” indicators.
- Report issues to organisers at registration immediately.

Consequences may include removal and escalation to venue security/authorities.  
Full reporting process will be published before RSVPs open.
